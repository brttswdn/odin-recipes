Scrambled Eggs

Home

Description

Scrambled eggs are a simple, healthy meal option. They are easily modified with a variety of seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Spices such as oregano or rosemary

Steps

  1. Heat small amount of oil in a saute pan
  2. Crack and mix eggs in a small mixing bowl.
  3. When pan is at medium-high heat, add eggs and garlic, stirring regularly.
  4. When eggs are cooked, slide eggs out of the pan and season as desired.
